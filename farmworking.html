How It Works for Farmers

⿡ Home

After login, you'll see your dashboard with a summary of products, orders, and earnings.

⿢ Add Product

Click on "Add Product" to list vegetables, fruits, milk, or other items. Fill in the name, price, quantity, and upload a photo.

⿣ My Product

This section shows all the products you've added. You can update or delete items from here anytime.

⿤ Orders

See new customer orders. Each order will show the product, quantity, price, and customer name. You can accept or reject orders.

⿥ Delivery Status

Track the status of deliveries — whether the order is pending, out for delivery, or completed.

⿦ Earning

Check your total earnings. You can view daily, weekly, or monthly income reports here.

⿧ Rate

See the ratings given by customers. Higher ratings improve your chances of getting more orders.

⿨ Logout

Click here to safely log out of your account when you're done.
